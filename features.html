Product Description

The Michael Kors Oversized Slim Runway Men's Watch exemplifies modern elegance with its sleek black design. This sophisticated timepiece features a round 44mm stainless steel case paired with a black dial that creates a bold, monochromatic look. The mineral crystal face provides excellent scratch resistance, ensuring your watch maintains its pristine appearance.

The black stainless steel bracelet band measures 22mm in width and can be customized to fit your wrist perfectly by removing links as needed. Powered by reliable quartz movement, this watch offers precise timekeeping with its three-hand analog display.

With water resistance up to 50 meters (165 feet), this watch is suitable for swimming in shallow water and has a 5 ATM rating. The versatile design of the Slim Runway watch makes it an ideal accessory for both casual and formal occasions, effortlessly elevating any outfit.

Whether you're attending a business meeting, enjoying a night out, or simply going about your daily activities, this Michael Kors timepiece adds a touch of sophistication to your look. Its timeless design ensures it will remain a stylish accessory for years to come.
